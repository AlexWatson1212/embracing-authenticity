  <div class="start-intro">
    <p class="lead">
      Self-discovery isn't about becoming someone else.
    </p>

    <p>
      It is about slowly returning to who you were before survival became your full-time job.
    </p>

    <p>
      This journey is designed to be gentle, practical, and grounded in real life.
      You do not need to complete it perfectly.
      You do not need to rush.
      You only need to begin where you are.
    </p>
  </div>
