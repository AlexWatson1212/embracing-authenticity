      Creativity is often self-discovery in disguise.
    </p>

      Long before we can explain ourselves, we often express ourselves.
    </p>

    <p>
      Through music, writing, art, photography, stories, movement, and imagination, parts of us begin to speak.
    </p>

  </div>

    <h2>Creativity is not performance.</h2>

    <p>
      Many people stop creating because they think creativity requires talent.
    </p>

    <p>
      It doesn't.
    </p>

    <p>
      Creativity begins the moment you become curious about your own experience.
    </p>

  </div>
