<section class="start-page">

  <div class="start-intro">

    <p class="lead">
      Understanding feelings is not the same as feeling them.
    </p>

    <p>
      Many thoughtful people become experts at analysing emotions while remaining disconnected from actually experiencing them.
    </p>

    <p>
      This isn't failure.
      Often it's protection.
    </p>

  </div>

  <div class="start-soft-block">

    <p class="home-kicker">Step 3</p>

    <h2>The shift</h2>

    <p>
      Instead of asking:
    </p>

    <blockquote>
      Why do I feel this?
    </blockquote>

    <p>
      Try asking:
    </p>

    <blockquote>
      Can I stay with this for a moment?
    </blockquote>

  </div>

  <div class="start-grid">

    <article class="start-card start-card--primary">
      <p class="home-card-step">Grief</p>
      <h3>Sadness often needs company</h3>

      <p>
        Many emotions soften when they are acknowledged rather than explained away.
      </p>
    </article>

    <article class="start-card">
      <p class="home-card-step">Fear</p>
      <h3>Anxiety usually has a message</h3>

      <p>
        Anxiety is often an attempt to protect you from uncertainty, vulnerability, or loss.
      </p>
    </article>

    <article class="start-card">
      <p class="home-card-step">Anger</p>
      <h3>Anger protects boundaries</h3>

      <p>
        Healthy anger often tells us something important matters.
      </p>
    </article>

    <article class="start-card">
      <p class="home-card-step">Longing</p>
      <h3>Longing reveals values</h3>

      <p>
        Sometimes what hurts most points toward what matters most.
      </p>
    </article>

  </div>

  <div class="start-guidance">

    <h2>A simple experiment</h2>

    <p>
      The next time an emotion appears:
    </p>

    <ul>
      <li>Name it.</li>
      <li>Notice where it lives in your body.</li>
      <li>Breathe.</li>
      <li>Stay curious.</li>
      <li>Allow it to move at its own pace.</li>
    </ul>

  </div>

  <div class="start-soft-block">

    <h2>Before you move on</h2>

    <p>
      Emotions are not problems to solve.
      They are experiences to move through.
    </p>
