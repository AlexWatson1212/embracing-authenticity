<p class="lead">
You don’t have to “sort your life out” to be here.
You just have to be willing to take one small, honest step toward yourself.
</p>

<p>
If you’ve been surviving for a long time, healing can feel like another pressure:
more to fix, more to understand, more to get right.
This space isn’t about that.
It’s about giving your nervous system, your emotions, and your relationships
a little more room to breathe — alongside therapy, or while you’re waiting for it.
</p>

<h2>Want Something More Structured?</h2>

<p>
If you like gentle structure, you can take the
<a href="/self-discovery/" class="inline-link">Self-Discovery Path</a> —
a slower journey through safety, feelings, identity, and integration.
You don’t have to do it “right” or complete every section.
Treat it like a toolbox, not a programme.
</p>

<hr />

<h2>How to Use This Site Without Overwhelming Yourself</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Read one thing at a time — then pause.</li>
  <li>Notice what lands in your body: softening, breath, tears, resistance.</li>
  <li>Take breaks when you feel foggy, tense, or over-stimulated.</li>
  <li>Bring anything important into your own therapy, journaling, or trusted conversations.</li>
</ul>

<p>
You don’t have to become a different person to be “doing the work”.
Staying kind and honest with yourself, one small decision at a time, is enough.
</p>
